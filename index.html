About me

Hi, I'm Steven — a data analyst and automation developer focused on building clean, reliable data pipelines and analytics tools that make teams faster and more accurate. I specialize in Python, SQL, and Snowflake, and I've spent the last two years delivering automation solutions across financial reporting, capital markets analytics, and enterprise data management.

I like solving messy, ambiguous problems. Whether it's reconciling metadata across business divisions, creating automated accounting workflows, or designing dashboards that simplify complex trends, I enjoy turning raw, inconsistent data into something structured, intuitive, and actionable. My work often involves designing ETL pipelines, writing automation scripts, building GUIs for internal users, and developing dashboards that help leadership make clearer decisions.

I'm especially interested in roles where analytics and engineering meet — places where I can combine Python automation, SQL, and business logic to create systems that scale. I enjoy understanding how data flows through an organization, identifying bottlenecks, and building tools that eliminate manual steps or reduce risk.

Right now, I'm focused on growing as an analyst in environments where I can work on deeper modeling, forecasting, capital markets analytics, or technical data workflows. I want to contribute to teams where the impact is tangible: faster reporting, cleaner data architecture, better decision frameworks, and more automated processes.

My approach is simple: build things that work, build them cleanly, and make the people around me faster.
